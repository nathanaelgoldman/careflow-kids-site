CareFlow Kids + CareView Kids

A paired workflow for clinicians and families built around a portable .pemr bundle. The clinician updates the record in CareFlow Kids. Families open the same bundle in CareView Kids to read reports, view growth charts, access documents, and send updated information back.

Join TestFlight Read documentation

How it works

Doctor uses CareFlow Kids
↓ export .pemr
Family opens bundle in CareView Kids
↓ notes / documents / re-export
Clinician re-imports updated bundle in CareFlow Kids

Two apps, one portable record

The system is designed around a cumulative patient bundle rather than a permanent shared cloud account.

CareFlow Kids

  • Create sick visits and well visits
  • Record vitals and growth data
  • Generate patient reports on demand
  • Export portable .pemr bundles

CareView Kids

  • Open bundles produced by CareFlow Kids
  • Read visit reports and view growth charts
  • Access uploaded documents
  • Add parent notes and export updated bundles

Key principles

  • Local-first architecture
  • Portable cumulative patient bundle
  • No cloud account required for bundle exchange
  • Readable family-facing reports

What families can do

  • Open bundles produced by CareFlow Kids
  • Read visit reports and view growth charts
  • Access and add documents
  • Add parent notes and return the updated bundle to the clinician
